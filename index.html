About Us

World's Biggest University

We’re a leading research university with a heart. Founded in the decade that the U.S. Constitution was signed, we’re the nation’s oldest Catholic and Jesuit university. Today we’re a forward-looking, diverse community devoted to social justice, restless inquiry and respect for each person’s individual needs and talents..

Our Facilities

We commit to meet the financial need of every admitted undergraduate student. We don’t consider your ability to pay when we review your application, and we don’t base our decision on whether you can cover the cost. If you’re accepted here, you belong here.

World Class Library

Our world-class library is the heart of academic resources, offering an extensive collection of books, journals, digital materials, and research databases. Equipped with quiet study zones, group discussion rooms, and modern technology, it provides an ideal space for learning, research, and collaboration.

Largest Playground

Our university boasts the largest playground in the region, designed to cater to a variety of sports and recreational activities. From basketball courts and football fields to running tracks, it provides students with ample opportunities to stay active, compete, and unwind.

Tasty and Healthy Food

Our cafeteria offers a wide variety of tasty and nutritious food options to suit all preferences and dietary needs. With fresh ingredients, diverse menus, and a welcoming atmosphere, it’s the perfect place for students to relax, socialize, and recharge during their busy day.
